employees-
       1. api/employees/getallemployees -retrieve data of all employees  
           -authorization Incharge only

       2. api/employees/GetEmployee/{id} - retrieve data of employee by their id
          -authorization Incharge, Store managers, Supervisors, store worker 

        3.api/employees/UpdateEmployee/{id} - Update data of employee by their id -fromBody Employee's data
         -authorization Incharge only

        4.api/employees/Insertemployee -  Insert data of employee  -fromBody Employee's data 
        -authorization Incharge only

        5.api/employees/deleteEmployee/{id} -  delete data of employee by their id 
          -authorization Incharge only

Materials-
       1. api/Materials/getallmaterials -retrieve data of all materials  
           -authorization Store managers, Incharge, Supervisors

       2. api/materials/getmaterial/{id} - retrieve data of material by their id
          -authorization Store managers, Incharge, Supervisors

        3.api/materials/updateMaterial/{id} - Update data of material by their id -fromBody material's data 
        -authorization Store managers only

        4.api/materials/addmaterial -  Insert data of Material  -fromBody material's data 
        -authorization Store managers only

        5.api/materials/deletematerial/{id} -  delete data of material by their id 
          -authorization Store managers only


Orders-
       1. api/Orders/ordershistory/{id} -retrieve data of all orders ordered by employee(ORDERS HISTORY OF SUPERVISOR)
           -authorization  Incharge, Supervisors, Store managers

       2. api/Orders/allordershistory - retrieve data of all orders ordered by all employees(HISTORY OF ORDERS OF ALL SUPERVISORS )
          -authorization  Incharge, Store managers

        1. api/Orders/orderdetails -retrieve data of order-details ordered by employee and location of material in warehouse(NEW TASK FOR STORE WORKER)
           -authorization   store worker

       2. api/Orders/orderdetailsHistory/{id} -retrieve data of all order-details delivered by worker.(ALL TASKS DONE BY STORE WORKER)
          -authorization  Incharge, Store managers, Store worker
